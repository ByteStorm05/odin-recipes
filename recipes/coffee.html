Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Milk
  • Whipped Cream
  • Java Chip Chocolate Chips
  • Coffee
  • Sweetened Whipped Cream
  • Chocolate Drizzle

Directions:

  1. Fill a blender with ice cubes.
  2. Pour in milk until it reaches the level of the ice.
  3. Add whipped cream, java chip chocolate chips, and coffee.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour into a glass and top with sweetened whipped cream.
  6. Drizzle with chocolate for extra flavor.
